Wind Speed vs Latitude



The program selected a random set of 1500 latitude and longitude measurements, from which we mapped to the city nearest these coordinates (and then eliminated any repeat cities from the dataset). There does not appear to be much correlation between the Latitue and Wind Speed. While this may be anomalous, it is interesting to note that Wind Speeds > 30 mph are only seen in Latitudes > +/- 25 degrees. It would be interesting to explore further if there is a jet stream affect that results in consistently higher wind speeds at the higher Northern and Southern latitudes.
